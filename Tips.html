Water Conservation Tips

  • Fix leaking faucets and pipes to prevent water wastage.
  • Install water-saving showerheads and flow restrictors.
  • Take shorter showers to reduce water usage.
  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth or shaving.
  • Collect rainwater for watering plants and gardens.
  • Water your lawn and garden in the early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation.
  • Use a broom instead of a hose to clean driveways and sidewalks.
  • Only run your dishwasher and washing machine with full loads.
